About Our School

A Legacy of Academic Excellence

Founded in Tsafe, Zamfara State, Darul Hikma International College has grown from a modest secondary school into one of the most respected educational institutions in North-West Nigeria. Our commitment to holistic education — combining academic rigour with moral development — has produced thousands of successful graduates serving Nigeria and beyond.

Mission: To provide quality, inclusive education that equips students with knowledge, skills, and values for sustainable development.

Vision: To be the leading secondary school in Nigeria, producing confident, compassionate, and competent graduates.

Admissions 2025/2026

How to Apply

Follow these simple steps to secure your child's place at DARUL HIKMA INTERNATIONAL COLLEGE.

1

Download or Collect Form

Pick up the application form from our school office or download it from the portal. Form fee: ₦2,000

2

Complete the Application

Fill in all sections carefully including academic records, passport photo, and guardian information.

3

Entrance Examination

All applicants sit a computer-based entrance exam covering English, Mathematics, and General Knowledge.

4

Interview & Offer

Shortlisted candidates are called for an oral interview. Successful applicants receive admission letters within 2 weeks.

5

Enrolment & Payment

Complete enrolment by paying the first-term school fees and collecting your student ID and timetable.

📋 School Fees (Per Term)

JS1 – JS3: ₦45,000 SS1 – SS3: ₦55,000 Science Class Extra: ₦8,000 Boarding (Optional): ₦35,000

* Fees are subject to review. Scholarships available for top-performing students.
